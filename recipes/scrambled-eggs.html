Scrambled Eggs

Description

This recipe for scrambled eggs requires a nonstick pan, eggs, butter or olive oil, and any desired seasonings/toppings for 1-2 servings and a total cook time of 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil or butter
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • Any desired toppings (shredded cheese, chopped chives, ham, etc.)

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into bowl and mix with fork or whisk until yolk and whites are combined.
  2. Add butter/oil to pan and bring to medium heat.
  3. Pour eggs in pan and begin to slowly stir in circles after a few seconds.
  4. Lower heat to medium-low and continue stirring slowly.
  5. When eggs are mostly set, turn off heat and add seasoning and toppings. Enjoy!
