Macaroni and Cheese

This macaroni and cheese recipe requires a large pot, saucepan, box macaroni, butter, flour, salt, pepper, milk, and shredded cheese for a total cook time of 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  • 0.25 cup butter
  • 0.25 cup flour
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • Pinch of black pepper (to taste)
  • 2 cups milk

Steps

  1. Bring salted water to boil in pot and cook macaroni until cooked but not firm (about 8 minutes).
  2. While macaroni cooks, melt butter in saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth (about 5 minutes).
  4. Pour in milk and continue stirring until mixture is smooth (about 5 minutes).
  5. Add cheese and stir until melted.
  6. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated. Enjoy!
